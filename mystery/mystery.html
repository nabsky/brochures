How Players Join the Jackpot

Bonus Bet Layout
Each table position includes a dedicated bonus bet area. Boxes follow the same 1–9 keypad layout used by the dealer for quick and intuitive entry. Table felt layouts can be supplied by us or provided as digital artwork.

One Bonus Bet per Round

Players participate by placing a fixed bonus bet on one or more table positions. Each participating position becomes a separate entry in the jackpot draw for the current round.

Dealer Confirmation

The dealer marks the participating boxes using the wireless keypad. Once all bonus bets are collected, the dealer confirms the entries and sends them into the draw with a single action.

Clear Participation Logic

Filled circles indicate newly accepted entries, while outlined circles show the boxes that have just been sent into the draw. This gives players and staff a clear visual understanding of which entries are currently participating.

Simple and Transparent

The participation flow is designed to be immediately understandable for both dealers and players. It requires minimal dealer actions while clearly showing who is involved in the current jackpot round.
